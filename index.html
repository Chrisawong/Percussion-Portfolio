    • About Me

    I am a percussionist based in the Greater Toronto Area, specializing in orchestral music. I have performed with numerous organizations such as the Greater Toronto Philharmonic Orchestra, Orchestra Toronto, Kindred Spirits Orchestra, and Cathedral Bluffs Symphony Orchestra.

    I’ve had the opportunity to perform alongside renowned soloists like Jonathan Crow, Yolanda Bruno, and Rame Lahaj, as well as conductors including Charles Cozens, David Fallis, and Martin MacDonald. Notable performances include the Yellow River Cantata 85th Anniversary Premiere, the world premiere of Canadian Composer Joel Toews’ Fanfare 401, the Embassy of Kosovo’s 25th-anniversary event commemorating Canada’s support for 7000 refugees, and a holiday concert hosted by the Embassy of the Republic of Albania.

    I hold an Honours Bachelor of Science in Mathematics with a Minor in Music, and currently study privately with Dr. Michelle Colton. Beyond percussion, I am a drum kit player and band leader at Toronto Community Alliance Church, and was an Electric Bassist for the Concert Band Association of Ryerson University.
